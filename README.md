# swordThing
Modrinth Minecraft Mod
Dive into a world of zero effort with this basic expansion made to break your Minecraft game right from the start. Whether you are a lazy player or just someone who hates trying, this mod turns the overworld into a place where you never have to work for anything. By completely removing the gap between spawning and winning, it ensures that your path to the end is incredibly short and requires no skill.Stumble through a cluttered ecosystem full of strange mobs that don't quite fit into the biomes. To help skip every challenge, the mod adds a collection of overpowered weapons and unbalanced armor sets made from a common, glowing ore. This shortcut resource serves as an easy way to bypass progression, offering an unearned alternative to traditional materials. For those who want to reach the end immediately, a simple new dimension awaits—a realm filled with free riches specifically designed to be accessible without any effort.Every aspect of this mod is built on making things as easy as possible. Dozens of simplified crafting recipes have been added so items can be obtained with minimal resources, removing the need for exploration and keeping the focus on instant gratification. By eliminating the grind and ignoring game balance, this mod turns the progression of Minecraft into a flat, effortless experience. A brief adventure starts here—faster, easier, and more unbalanced than ever before.
